About Me:

You're here because you want to know more about who I am. As you probably already know, my name is Hillary. I was born and raised in Southern California. I went to college at UCLA and later earned my Master's degree in English from Loyola Marymount university. In 2013 I moved to Portland, Oregon with my husband, Dave, and our cat, Ella Fitzgerald. In my free time I like to enjoy all Portland has to offer by going to local shows, eating delicious pastries, going for hikes, and reading lots of books in coffee shops or parks.

Let me tell you a little about my professional background. I worked in academia for nearly a decade, first as an advisor and later as an English instructor. So how did I get into coding? I first became interested in coding as an advisor when I was asked to update the program websites using very basic HTML tags. Then, I started exprimenting with more coding and basic css by creating simple web pages for my English courses. I enjoyed the work so much that I began to think more seriously about learning to code. It took several years to convince myself that I wanted to make a full-time career change. So, in 2015, I took a chance and enrolled at Epicodus where I'm now learning to code at lightning speed. Eventually I hope to go into web development and perhaps UX design. I'm excited about this new journey and I hope you enjoy looking over my projects!
